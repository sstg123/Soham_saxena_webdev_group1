What is Coronavirus?

Coronavirus also known as Sars-Cov-2 or Covid-19 is a deadly disease which impacts your lungs and respiratory system in the body. People affected with Covid-19, might have symptoms like:

1. Coughing
2. Fever
3. Mild respiratory disease.

Older people with diabetes, cancer, chronic respiratory disease might have more impact from this virus. The virus spreads through droplets of saliva or cough which comes out from the nose when an infected person coughs or sneezes. There are some precautions that you need to take, to stop the spread of this deadly virus:

1. Cough with a flexed elbow.
2. If you experience any of the symptoms above, please take a Covid-19 test or stay isolated at home for 14 days.
3. Maintain at least a 1-3 meters distance, if you are meeting anyone. This is social distancing.
4. Wear a mask if you go outside of your house at all points in time.


Vaccine Update... There are about 100 Covid-19 vaccine candidates under development. WHO is trying to collaborate with other such labaratories and organizations. When an effective vaccine is found, the COVAX(led by WHO, GAVI and CEPI), will transport these vaccines to other such countries.
